Thrissur


Thrissur is also known as the "cultural capital of Kerala" because of its cultural, spiritual and religious leanings throughout history. The name Thrissur(Thrishiva Perur) is derived from 'Thiru-Shiva-Perur' (Tamil), which translates to "The city with the name of the Lord Siva". It is also called the land of Poorams. The district is known for its ancient temples, churches, and mosques. Thrissur Pooram is the most colourful temple festival in Kerala.

Here are some places to visit:

  • Vadakkumnathan Temple
  • Chavakkad Beach
  • Kerala Kalamandalam
  • Shakthan Thampuran Palace
  • Paramekavu Bhagavathy Temple
