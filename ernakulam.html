Ernakulam


Ernakulam is located in the central part of Kerala and includes Kochi city, which is known as the commercial capital of state. The district is famous for its ancient temples, churches, and mosques. The district includes the largest metropolitan region of the state, Greater Cochin. Soaked in history and the many different grandeurs of Kerala, Ernakulam is one of the most stunning destinations in God’s Own Country! While the existence of this city dates back to the Stone Age, today it is revered to have the most electrifying ambience in the entire country. Ancient, yet with modern charm, Ernakulam also serves as a throbbing hub for budding entrepreneurs and also contributes significantly to the state’s tourism industry.

Here are some places to visit:

  • Mangalavanam Bird Sanctuary
  • Mattanchery Palace
  • Folklore Museum
  • Athirapally Waterfalls
  • Cherai Beach
