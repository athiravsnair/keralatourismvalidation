Palakkad


Palakkad district has a large number of temples, churches, mosques and other tourist attractions. It is situated in the middle of Kerala state in South India. Main tourist attraction is Palakkad fort in the heart of the city and Malampuzha Dam and Malampuzha gardens (15 km away). Near Malampuzha gardens are Fantasy park (a water theme amusement park), a ropeway, snake park and a beautiful aquarium.Malampuzha also has South India's first rock garden[citation needed]. Ahalia Heritage village is an attraction near Kanjikode. Kalpathy Heritage village is the main tourist attraction in the heart of the city. Numerous temples like Hemambika temple, Kodumbu temple, Manappullikkavu Devi temple etc attract tourists too.

Here are some places to visit:

  • Nelliyampathy
  • Parambikulam Tiger Reserve
  • Malampuzha Dam
  • Seethargundu Viewpoint
  • Kalpathy Temple
